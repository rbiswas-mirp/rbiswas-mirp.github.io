I started my journey with blogging on the world's most popular blogging platform - WordPress. Soon I realised that there are many guides for any topic but none of them were accurate. I was surprised to find that even after so many years of WordPress being in the market, the guides were not good enough for a beginner. So, on April 2020, I started WPSteam. WPSteam is a tutorial site for beginner and advanced users of WordPress and AWS (AWS!?) yes AWS. So, what is AWS? AWS is a cloud computing platform made by Amazon. Why did I decide to make guides for AWS too? Well, AWS is the best place to host a WorsPress site or for that matter - any site (fun fact! WPSteam is hosted on AWS!). So, feel free to explore my personal website and also make sure to visit WPSteam (who knows! You might want to start blogging by reading a few articles on WPSteam!).
